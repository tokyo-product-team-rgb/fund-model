What We Cannot Verify

⚠️ Not Publicly Disclosed

The following data points are proprietary and not reported by major VC firms:

CategoryWhat We Don't KnowWhy It Matters
Scout Programs Exact $ allocated, # scouts, carry terms, deal volume Hard to compare sourcing strategies
Fund Construction % % to seed vs. A vs. growth vs. follow-on reserves Can't model portfolio construction precisely
Ownership Targets Specific % targets by stage, minimum thresholds Industry estimates vary widely (10-25%)
Deals Per Year Total deal flow, conversion rates, pass rates Can't assess selectivity or bandwidth
GP Economics Management fee %, carry splits, GP commit Standard "2 and 20" varies significantly
LP Base Which endowments, pensions, family offices invest Only disclosed when LPs file publicly
Decision Process # partners to approve, IC structure, veto rights Affects speed and deal dynamics
Win Rates % of deals pursued vs. won, competitive dynamics Can't assess brand/access advantage
IRR/TVPI by Fund Only Benchmark's 7.5x is public Most returns data is estimated

💡 Industry Standard Estimates

These are commonly cited but not verified firm-by-firm:

MetricIndustry EstimateReality
Management Fee 2% of committed capital Often 1.5-2.5%, may step down over time
Carried Interest 20% of profits Top firms may command 25-30%
GP Commit 1-2% of fund Some firms commit 5%+
Follow-on Reserves 40-60% of fund Varies by strategy (Benchmark: ~53%)
Ownership Target 15-20% at Series A Ranges from 10-25%+ depending on firm
Fund Life 10 years + 2 extensions Some permanent capital (Sequoia)

Note: Any analysis using these percentages should treat them as directional estimates, not verified facts.
